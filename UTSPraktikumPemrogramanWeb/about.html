About Us

Rooted in a deep passion for the visual arts, we are a professional wedding photography and videography service. With a strong background in multimedia and visual storytelling, we are dedicated to weaving your precious moments into timeless, cinematic masterpieces. We offer a variety of wedding documentation packages carefully designed to meet the needs of both domestic and international couples.

Our Services

  1. All-In Package (Complete Documentation)

    In the All-In package, we provide comprehensive photography and videography coverage, from preparation and the holy matrimony/ceremony to the end of the wedding reception. It is the perfect choice to ensure not a single precious second is missed on your special day.

  2. Holy Matrimony / Ceremony Package

    The main focus of this package is to immortalize the sacredness of your core wedding ceremony. Our team will capture every emotion, tear of joy, and sacred vow with intimate, profound, and cinematic details.

  3. Pre-Wedding or Engagement Package

    Before the big day arrives, we help you visualize your love story through a pre-wedding photo and video session. We are ready to execute various concepts, whether in-studio or through memorable outdoor explorations.

  4. Intimate Wedding Package

    This package is specifically designed for small-scale, intimate weddings, typically attended only by family and closest relatives. Our approach focuses on a journalistic style to document the warmth of your moments naturally and candidly.

  5. Same-Day Edit (SDE) Video Service

    For those who want to surprise their guests, we offer an SDE service. Our editing team will quickly process the video highlights of the morning events (preparation and ceremony) to be screened directly during the reception on the same day.

  6. Custom Package

    We understand that every wedding has different needs and scales. This custom package allows you to adjust the duration of coverage, the number of photographers/videographers, and even add extra services like aerial drone coverage or exclusive printed albums.
